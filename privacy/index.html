Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Who we are

ZapPod is a daily audio briefing app delivering multi‑topic news summaries via audio only. Contact: zappod.info@gmail.com.

Data we collect

  • Account data: email address for sign‑in and account management (via Supabase Auth).
  • App diagnostics: basic crash logs and performance metrics to improve stability.
  • Usage preferences: basic app settings (e.g., playback preferences) stored locally for your convenience.

We do not sell personal data. We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, or microphone recordings.

How we use data

  • Provide the service (e.g., deliver daily audio briefings across multiple topics).
  • Secure accounts and prevent abuse.
  • Fix bugs, analyze crashes, and improve performance.

Data sharing

We share data only with service providers necessary to operate ZapPod (e.g., authentication, hosting, analytics). These providers process data on our behalf under contractual safeguards.

Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed to provide the service or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account data at any time.

Your choices

  • Access, update, or delete your account by contacting us at zappod.info@gmail.com.
  • Opt out of non‑essential emails by using unsubscribe links.
  • Disable analytics/diagnostics in app settings (if available), or contact us.

Children

ZapPod is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We use safeguards where required by law.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated date at the top of this page.
